About

Growing up, I was drawn to the piano. At the age of 11 I posted a cover of the song All of Me by Jon Schmidt which garnered over 30,000 views. This sparked an interest in creating my own music as I saw my own potential. For years, my only method of recording were "voice memos," a tool many young composer are well familiar with.

My songs all have some story, emotion, or imagery that enhance the composing process. It's all good for study, relaxation, inspiration, and maybe even perspiration.. ya know, like running or something.

A long-time friend of mine Jacob Howell helped me compose my first album. He is a wicked good guitarist/composer who co-creates with me on the piano.

Hope you enjoy the music!

Albums

Jacob Howell, my college roommate, wrote half of the songs on guitar first. He is the inspired me to create my first album. That's also why 2 of these songs are named "Guitar 1" and "Guitar 2".

A lot of these songs are inspired by my family. The song Madison expresses gratitude to my wife, Madison. When I asked my youngest cousin, who is 8 years old, what my song reminded him of - he said "wolves" - thus "Return of the Wolves" was born.
